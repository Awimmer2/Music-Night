What is Pop Music?

Pop music, short for "popular music," is a genre known for its catchy melodies, easy-to-follow lyrics, and mass appeal. It emerged in its modern form during the mid-20th century and has evolved by blending elements of rock, dance, hip-hop, and electronic music.

Pop songs typically focus on themes like love, relationships, fun, and emotional experiences, often structured in a verse-chorus format that's designed to stay in your head.

Some of the most iconic pop artists include Michael Jackson, often called the "King of Pop," as well as Madonna, Britney Spears, Lady Gaga, Taylor Swift, and Dua Lipa.

Today, pop continues to dominate charts worldwide, constantly evolving through collaborations with other genres and new digital sounds.
